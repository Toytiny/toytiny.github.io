about me

I am a final-year PhD student from the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh, sponsored by the EPSRC CDT in Robotics and Autonomous Systems, supervised by Prof. Chris Xiaoxuan Lu and Prof. Barbara Webb.

🙏 I will graduate in Summer 2025 and am actively seeking a postdoctoral or industrial position, starting from my graduation.

My research focuses on advancing the robustness, efficiency, and privacy-awarness of perception systems for embodied AI. These efforts support applications in mobile robots, autonomous vehicles, AIoT and xR-integrated systems, aiming to facilitate their widespread, long-horizon and non-intrusive deployment in the wild. Currently, my works primarily involve the imaging modalities that operate beyond visible spectrum, e.g., mmWave radar and thermal, leveraging their unique sensing resilience to cope with adverse weather, variable illumination and occlusion challenges in real world.

Before coming to Edinburgh, I received my B.Eng degree with the highest honour - Academic Star and was awarded double National Scholarships from Tongji University (2017-2021).

👉 I am looking for self-motivated UG/Ms students to work with me and my collaborators at UoE/UCL/ETH/MIT/Cambridge/NTU on cutting-edge research projects.

Support in the form of sufficient computational and sensory tools, robot platform, alongside practical supervision and direction, is available. Please fill the form here if you are interested.

👉 I am actively looking for a research intern position this year. Shoot me an email if you think I am a good fit!
